What do the badges next to each post mean?

Credibility shows how much you — through people you trust, and people they trust — can vouch for this author. "Unrated" means you have no trust path to them yet. It does not mean they are distrusted.

Boost shows how many sats other people have spent to promote this post. It fades over time, and is usually zero right now since boosting isn't fully wired up yet.

Karma shows how much the people you trust have liked this post — a stranger's like counts for nothing to you, only people on your own trust graph move this number. Liking a post is also a public, signed statement under your own identity, not an anonymous click.
